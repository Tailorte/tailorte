Tailorte App Features

Business Management

Tailorte allows tailors to manage their business, including creating and editing their profile, setting prices, managing orders, and tracking sales and revenue.

Product Catalog

Tailors can upload photos of their products, including clothing, accessories, and fabrics, and create a catalog to showcase their work to potential customers.

Customer Management

Tailorte helps tailors keep track of their customers, including contact information, measurements, and order history.

For Fashion Lovers

Features specifically designed to help fashion lovers discover new trends, purchase products, and connect with tailors

Trend Discovery

Users can browse a variety of fashion trends and styles, curated by Tailorte's team of experts and other users. They can also follow their favorite tailors to see new products and styles.

Measurement Sharing

Users can easily share their measurements with tailors, making it easier to order custom clothing that fits perfectly.
